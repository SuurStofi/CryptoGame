Frequently Asked Questions

How to play Monster Cocktail?

Connect Phantom Wallet to the game on app.monster-cocktail.com. Catch monsters, collect ingredients and create cocktails for boosts or sell them on the marketplace for SOL!

What are cocktails and how to use them?

Cocktails are tokens on Solana that you can use as boosts in the game for more efficient monster catching or sell to other players for SOL on the marketplace.

Can I earn SOL?

Yes! Caught monsters allow you to create unique cocktail-tokens that can be sold to other players for SOL on the marketplace.

What network does the project use?

Monster Cocktail runs on Solana Devnet, allowing you to test functionality without real money.
